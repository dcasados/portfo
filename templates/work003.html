Buy with Opendoor


Background.

Upon post-merger integration, Buy with Opendoor's key objectives were to develop and launch a full-stack homebuying service to expand OD's product suite, establish product/market fit, and launch 6 major metros in Y1. Dillon scaled the operation team's business intelligence and P&L management frameworks to drive GTM strategy and OKR/KPI development. Thereafter, he led a small sales team that radically increased user conversion and revenue, which inspired a new product feature that he oversaw the operations on through its development.

Key Learnings.

  • Ideas are only as good as the clarity of their presentation.
  • A good GTM strategy optimizes for quality, not quantity.
  • Good leadership flourishes through radical candor.
  • Maintain a work/life balance for optimal output.
  • Rapid growth requires great playbooks.
  • Invest in impeccable data insights.
  • Understand your customer's needs.
  • Have fun!
